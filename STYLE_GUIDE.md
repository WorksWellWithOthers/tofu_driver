# Tofu Driver Style Guide

## Voice

Use calm, safety-aware language. The app should feel playful, but never imply that driving faster is
better or that the phone should be watched while driving.

Preferred terms:

- Tofu Driver
- The Cup Test
- smooth driving
- water delivered
- practice run
- qualified run
- local summary
- share result

Avoid:

- race
- fastest
- beat traffic
- high-speed
- pro driver
- certified safe
- guaranteed safe
- insurance
- legal compliance

## UI Copy

- Keep instructions short.
- Make safety warnings direct.
- Use “Do not look at the screen while driving.”
- Say when location is optional.
- Say when data stays local.

## Visual Direction

- Mobile-first.
- Large touch targets.
- High contrast in outdoor lighting.
- Stable layouts that do not shift during a run.
- Avoid dense dashboards while the run is active.

## Result Language

Results should emphasize smoothness and completion, not speed.

Good:

- “You delivered 97% of the cup.”
- “Smooth run.”
- “Qualified run complete.”

Avoid:

- “Fastest run.”
- “Beat your route.”
- “Drive harder.”
